Common Ground Conference

Branding for a conference that fosters inter-cultural, inter-ethnic, and inter-religious relationships internationally. Based on the idea of the legacy of stamps: no matter what background you have and no matter how it is represented, the idea is still prevalent through the dove and olive leaves—the idea of peace.

Ubu Roi Artist Book

An artist based on Alfred Jarry's "Ubu Roi." Ubu, a greedy and foolish idiot, manages to usurp his kingdom's throne. This artist book compares his bloody and gruesome journey to his preparing and cooking a meal.
